After the Rain

2018 | Romance · Drama · Slice of Life | 1 Season | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Jun Mayuzuki

Cast: Sayumi Watabe, Hiroaki Hirata, Mikako Komatsu

Akira Tachibana, a quiet former track star, loses her passion for running after an injury. Working part-time at a restaurant, she develops feelings for Masami Kondō, the gentle middle-aged manager. Despite the age difference, their connection helps them confront past regrets and rediscover their dreams.
