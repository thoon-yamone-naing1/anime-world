5 Centimeters per Second

2007 | Romance · Drama | 1h 3min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Makoto Shinkai

Cast: Kenji Mizuhashi, Masaki Terasoma, Chinami Nishimura

Takaki and Akari were close friends in elementary school, but are separated when Akari moves away. Over time, Takaki struggles with distance, longing, and memories of their time together, as they attempt to reconnect despite the miles between them.
