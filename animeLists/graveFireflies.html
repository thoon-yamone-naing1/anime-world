Grave of the Fireflies

1988 | Drama · War · Tragedy | 1h 29min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Isao Takahata

Cast: Tsutomu Tatsumi, Ayano Shiraishi, Yoshiko Shinohara

In the final months of WWII, Seita and his little sister Setsuko struggle to survive after a bombing raid destroys their home. Alone in an unforgiving world, they face the devastating realities of hunger and loss while trying to find moments of hope in the flickering light of fireflies.
