Kiki’s Delivery Service

1989 | Fantasy · Adventure · Slice of Life | 1h 43min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki

Cast: Minami Takayama, Rei Sakuma, Kappei Yamaguchi

At thirteen, the young witch Kiki leaves home to begin her training. Settling in a beautiful seaside town with her talking cat Jiji, she opens a flying delivery service. Through the highs and lows of independence, Kiki discovers the true meaning of confidence and inspiration.
