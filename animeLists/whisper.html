Whisper of the Heart

1995 | Romance · Drama · Slice of Life | 1h 51min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki

Cast: Yoko Honna, Issei Takahashi, Takashi Tachibana

Shizuku Tsukishima is a curious and imaginative middle school girl who loves reading and dreams of becoming a writer. She notices that many of the books she borrows were previously checked out by a boy named Seiji Amasawa. When they meet, Seiji inspires Shizuku to pursue her dreams seriously. Through her journey, she learns about hard work, self-discovery, and believing in herself.
