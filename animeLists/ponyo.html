Ponyo

2008 | Fantasy · Adventure · Family | 1h 41min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki

Cast: Yuria Nara, Hiroki Doi, Tomoko Yamaguchi

Fujimoto, a once-human wizard, lives under the sea with his daughter Ponyo, a goldfish who longs to become human. One day, Ponyo escapes and meets Sōsuke, a young boy who lives near the sea. As Ponyo transforms into a human girl, they embark on a magical adventure filled with wonder, danger, and the power of friendship and love, ultimately restoring balance between land and sea.
