When Marnie Was There

2014 | Drama · Mystery · Slice of Life | 1h 43min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hiromasa Yonebayashi

Cast: Sara Takatsuki, Kasumi Arimura, Nanako Matsushima

Anna Sasaki, a quiet and introverted girl, is sent to the countryside for her health. There, she meets Marnie, a mysterious girl who lives in a grand, old mansion. As their friendship deepens, Anna discovers secrets about Marnie’s past and her own family. Through this connection, Anna learns about love, acceptance, and understanding her own emotions.
