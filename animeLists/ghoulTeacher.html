A Terrified Teacher at Ghoul School!

2024 | Comedy · Supernatural · School | ~24 min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Takahiro Yamada

Based on: Manga by Mai Tanaka

Haruaki Abe is a timid teacher assigned to Hyakki Academy — a school filled entirely with yokai. Terrified of ghosts, he must survive teaching students far more powerful than him. Despite his fear, Haruaki slowly bonds with his unusual students while facing supernatural challenges.
