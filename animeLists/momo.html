A Letter to Momo

2011 | Drama · Fantasy · Family | 2h 0min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hiroyuki Okiura

Cast: Karen Miyama, Yūka, Toshiyuki Nishida

After the sudden death of her father, 11‑year‑old Momo Miyaura moves with her mother from Tokyo to a small remote island. Carrying her father’s unfinished letter that begins only with “Dear Momo,” she struggles to cope with her loss. One day, she encounters three mischievous goblin‑like spirits who help her begin to grow and heal.
