Wolf Children

2012 | Drama · Fantasy · Slice of Life | 1h 57min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Mamoru Hosoda

Cast: Aoi Miyazaki, Takao Osawa, Haru Kuroki, Yukito Nishii

Hana, a college student, falls in love with a werewolf and has two children, Yuki and Ame. After her partner's sudden death, she moves to the countryside to raise them alone. As the children grow, they must struggle with their dual identities and decide whether they want to live their lives as humans or as wolves.
