The Apothecary Diaries

2023–Present | Drama · Mystery · Historical · Romance | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Natsu Hyūga

Cast: Aoi Yūki, Takeo Ōtsuka, Katsuyuki Konishi

Maomao, a clever apothecary from the pleasure district, is kidnapped and sold into the Rear Palace. By using her vast knowledge of herbs and poisons, she saves the Emperor's heirs and finds herself entangled in the dangerous political intrigues of the court under the watchful eye of the beautiful official, Jinshi.
