Assassination Classroom

2015 | Action · Comedy · Sci-Fi · School | 2 Seasons | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Yusei Matsui

Cast: Jun Fukuyama, Mai Fuchigami, Nobuhiko Okamoto

A powerful creature destroys part of the moon and threatens Earth. Surprisingly, it becomes the teacher of Class 3-E. The students are tasked with assassinating their teacher, Koro-sensei, before graduation, learning valuable lessons about life and friendship along the way.
