Ocean Waves

1993 | Drama · Romance · Slice of Life | 1h 29min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Saeko Himuro

Cast: Rikako Aikawa, Ryo Horikawa, Tomoko Kawasaki

Taku Morisaki’s quiet high school life in Kochi is disrupted by the arrival of Rikako Muto, a beautiful transfer student from Tokyo. Their complicated relationship, filled with misunderstandings and unspoken feelings, captures the emotional turbulence of adolescence and the bittersweet nostalgia of growing up.
