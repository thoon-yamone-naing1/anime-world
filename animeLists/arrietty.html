The Secret World of Arrietty

2010 | Fantasy · Adventure · Family | 1h 34min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki, Keiko Niwa

Cast: Mirai Shida, Ryunosuke Kamiki, Shinobu Ōtake

Beneath the floorboards lives Arrietty, a tiny "Borrower" who survives by taking small items from humans. When she is discovered by a human boy named Shō, their secret friendship challenges the rules of her world and teaches them both the true meaning of courage and trust.
