My Neighbor Totoro

1988 | Fantasy · Family · Slice of Life | 1h 26min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki

Cast: Noriko Hidaka, Chika Sakamoto, Hitoshi Takagi

Two sisters move to the countryside to be near their sick mother. While exploring their new home, they discover a world of friendly forest spirits, led by the giant, cuddly Totoro. Together, they embark on magical adventures that bring comfort and joy during a time of uncertainty.
