Maquia:When the Promised Flower Blooms

2018 | Fantasy · Drama · Adventure | 1h 55min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Mari Okada

Cast: Manaka Iwami, Miyu Irino, Yuki Kaji

Maquia is a member of a long-lived race called the Iorph. After her home is attacked, she finds an orphaned human baby in the forest and decides to raise him as her own. As the boy, Ariel, grows while Maquia remains young, they face the challenges of time, war, and the bittersweet nature of maternal love.
