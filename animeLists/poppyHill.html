From Up on Poppy Hill

2011 | Romance · Drama · Slice of Life | 1h 31min | Anime | Japanese

Written by: Hayao Miyazaki, Keiko Niwa

Cast: Masami Nagasawa, Junichi Okada, Keiko Takeshita

In 1963 Yokohama, Umi raises signal flags every morning to honor her late father. When she meets Shun, a passionate student journalist, they team up to save their school's historic clubhouse. Together, they uncover family secrets and the depth of their connection.
